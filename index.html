About me

I'm a new Computer Science graduate from UC San Diego. Currently, I'm working as a Software Development Engineer at the Alexa Calling Experience Team, Amazon.com, Inc.

During my M.S. degree, I worked with Prof. Haijun Xia to study how people describe data through language. I was also involved in studying interactions and ways to reduce frictions in online meetings. I obtained a B.Eng. in Computer Engineering from Chulalongkorn University working with Prof. Boonserm Kijsirikul focusing on emotion recognition from brainwaves during music listening.

My research lies in the field of Human-Computer Interaction, Computer-Supported Cooperative Work, Machine Learning, and Artificial Intelligence. I'm interested in learning how people behave and build tools that help us be more productive.
